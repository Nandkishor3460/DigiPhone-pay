WHO WE ARE

At DIGI PHONE PAY , our idea is to make digital payment facilities affordable and feasible for everybody. Our products and services are aimed to facilitate all types of B2B and B2C transactions with utmost safety and speed. We not only complete all transactions at the least cost but also reward users with attractive and instant rewards. These rewards are a way to give back to the customers for using our platform as we believe that banking transactions are meant for everybody and there shouldn’t be any delay or hidden charges in it. Rewards and seasonal bonuses for various transaction options are regularly provided. This makes sure paying online is not the same anymore. Our payment gateways are built using the strongest security to ensure that your privacy is always respected. So that your information always remains unknown to the world.

In this race to make digital payment a norm within people, we aim to spread more and more of our technology in the rural and semi-urban areas of our country. This is so that we can successfully connect semi-rural and rural areas to make our country stronger and digitally advanced.

Together our team is working every second to make your payment gateway safer and faster than ever. And we are slowly climbing towards our dream of becoming the biggest payment solution provider in the country.
