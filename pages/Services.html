At DIGI PHONE PAY , we are committed to bridge the digital divide between urban & rural India by providing basic banking & financial services to everyone securely and instantly, in a hassle-free manner. DIGI PHONE PAY innovative banking solutions and extensive reach offer services of the Cash Deposit, Money Transfer & Cash Withdrawal to the under-served population of India through our strong agent network. We also provide the facility for bill payments, recharge and booking travel on bus, train, and air.

Account Opening

DIGI PHONE PAY Services allows users to open a digital bank account. Our trained agent will help you set up internet banking for your already existing bank account. Digital transactions can be conducted after AADHAR number verification and fingerprint authentication.

Domestic Money Transfer

With DIGI PHONE PAY Domestic Money Transfer service, retailers can help customers to instantly transfer their money to any bank account in the country, anywhere and anytime, even on bank holidays. The sender does not need a bank account to transfer the money from, and just need to pay a minimal transaction fee to the retailers.

Aadhar Enable Payment Service

Aadhaar Enabled Payment Service (AEPS), uses Aadhaar Data and Biometric authentication instead of signature or debit card information of the customers and allows them to quickly Withdraw, Deposit and Transfer cash & also access bank statements without visiting their bank branch. So, instead of searching for an ATM or a bank branch, customers can visit our nearest DIGI PHONE PAY retail Outlet and do all of these transactions using their Aadhaar Data and Biometric Authentication.
